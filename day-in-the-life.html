DAILY RHYTHM

A Typical Agent Day

Every agent's schedule is different, but most successful days are built around consistent activity and follow-up.

Morning

Plan The Day

Review your calendar, follow-ups, client notes, and training priorities. Decide who needs a call, quote, review, or reminder.

Midday

Talk To People

Reach out to prospects, respond to messages, schedule appointments, and have conversations with families who requested help.

Afternoon

Prepare Solutions

Compare options, gather information, prepare recommendations, and make sure families understand their choices clearly.

Evening

Meet And Follow Up

Many families are available after work. This is often when agents hold appointments, answer questions, and complete next steps.
