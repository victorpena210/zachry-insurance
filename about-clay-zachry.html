COVERAGE OPTIONS

Life Insurance Options Clay Can Help You Explore

Clay can help clients understand and compare several types of life insurance based on their goals, eligibility and available carrier products.

Term Life Insurance

Coverage designed to remain in effect for a selected period, subject to the policy’s terms and continued premium payments.

Whole Life Insurance

Permanent life insurance designed to provide lifelong coverage when required premiums are paid according to the policy’s terms.

Final Expense Whole Life

A form of whole life insurance generally designed to help beneficiaries manage funeral, burial and other end-of-life expenses.

Universal Life Insurance

Permanent life insurance that may offer flexible policy features. Costs, values and requirements vary by policy and carrier.

Product availability, features, rates and eligibility vary by state, carrier and applicant. Coverage is subject to underwriting, policy terms and approval.
