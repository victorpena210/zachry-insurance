COMMON QUESTIONS

Term Life Insurance FAQ

Answers to common questions from Austin families comparing term life insurance.

Term life insurance is coverage designed to remain in force for a selected period, subject to the policy's terms and continued premium payments. If the insured person dies while eligible coverage is active, the policy may pay a death benefit to the named beneficiaries.

The amount depends on your income, mortgage, debts, family responsibilities, existing coverage, and long-term goals. Zachry Insurance can help you review those needs before comparing options.

A term life policy can be structured with a benefit intended to help beneficiaries manage mortgage payments or other household expenses. The benefit is generally paid to the named beneficiary rather than directly to the mortgage lender.

Zachry Insurance works as an independent agency and can help clients compare available options from multiple carriers based on eligibility and coverage needs.
