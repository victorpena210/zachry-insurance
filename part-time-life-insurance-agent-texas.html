PART-TIME PATH

Can You Start Part-Time?

Some people explore life insurance part-time while keeping another job. The key is being honest about your schedule, staying consistent with follow-up, and treating training seriously even if your weekly hours are limited.

Time Blocks

Set aside focused blocks for calls, appointments, training, and follow-up.

Clear Expectations

Part-time does not mean casual. Families still need clear communication and consistent service.

Skill Building

Use each week to improve your confidence, product knowledge, and conversation skills.
